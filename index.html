Keg Share

Mobile App Prototype Design for iOS and Android Devices.

The “Keg Share” app was created as a prototype for MillerCoors where if the user creates their own group and purchases a keg of beer, they are able to share it among friends and use it at any participating bars around their area. They can customize their keg based off of their favorite sports team or group brand.

This prototype was completely designed and made into a real working app prototype that clients could show around and get the idea of what this app would eventually become. This app is currently in the works within MillerCoors to be created into a real app.

  • Client: Miller Coors

Star Servers

Mobile Design & Prototype

The “Star Servers” app was created as a prototype for Coors Light where the user can rate their favorite servers. Servers can also use the app to control their profile. The use of the app is to rate servers, find retailers near them with high rating servers, and also connect with their friends.

This prototype was completely designed and made into a real working app prototype that clients could show around and get the idea of what this app would eventually become. This app was killed after Miller Coors decided to go with the "Keg Share" app that was also created by me.
